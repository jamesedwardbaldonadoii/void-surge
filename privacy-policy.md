# Privacy Policy for Void Surge

**Last updated:** March 14, 2026

Void Surge collects minimal data to provide gameplay features.

When you create a profile, your chosen gamertag and game scores are stored on our servers to power the global leaderboard. We use Supabase for authentication, which may store a randomly generated user ID and session token.

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personal information beyond what is needed for leaderboard functionality.

- No data is shared with third parties
- No ads are served
- No analytics or tracking tools are used

If you wish to delete your data, contact us at jamesb@tawk.to.

This policy may be updated from time to time.
